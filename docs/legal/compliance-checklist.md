## Quick Reference — Key 2026 Thresholds (verify before relying)

- **US economic nexus (typical):** $100,000 in sales or 200 transactions per state (several states dropped the 200-transaction test).
- **EU IOSS cap:** €150 intrinsic value (still in force in 2026).
- **EU low-value customs duty:** €150 duty exemption removed from **1 July 2026**; transitional **€3 per tariff line item** added on top of VAT.
- **EU distance-selling (OSS) threshold:** €10,000.
- **UK VAT at checkout:** consignments £135 or under.
- **GDPR DSAR:** 30 days (+60). **CCPA DSAR:** 45 days (+45).
- **CAN-SPAM opt-out:** honor within 10 business days.
- **FTC influencer penalties:** can exceed ~$50,000 per violation.
- **COPPA:** no knowing collection from children under 13.
