## 1. How Our Fulfillment Works

Piece of Stass curates unbranded, "inspired-by" lifestyle goods sourced directly from carefully selected overseas manufacturing partners. To keep prices fair and our catalog fresh, many items ship **directly from our suppliers to you**. This means a slightly longer delivery window than big-box retailers — but it also means lower prices and access to pieces you won't find on every corner. We believe in being upfront about this so there are no surprises.

## 2. Order Processing Time

- **Processing time:** Orders are typically processed within **2–5 business days** after payment is confirmed. Processing includes order verification, quality checks, and dispatch preparation.
- Orders placed on weekends or holidays begin processing the next business day.
- During promotions, new launches, or peak seasons, processing may take a little longer; we'll keep you informed.

## 3. Transit (Delivery) Time

- **Transit time:** After dispatch, international transit typically takes **8–15 business days**, depending on destination and carrier.
- **Total estimated delivery time:** Combining processing and transit, most orders arrive within **10–20 business days** from the date of purchase.

> **Please note:** These are good-faith **estimates, not guarantees**. Actual delivery can vary due to customs clearance, carrier schedules, weather, remote destinations, or events outside our control. If we learn of a significant delay, we will contact you.

## 6. Customs, Duties, Import VAT & Taxes

Because many items ship internationally, **import duties, taxes, and fees may apply depending on your country's laws**, and you (the recipient) are generally responsible for them as the importer of record, unless we expressly collect them at checkout.

- **United States buyers:** Most low-value personal shipments enter under the U.S. **de minimis** framework, but rules and thresholds are subject to change by U.S. Customs and Border Protection. In some cases, duties, tariffs, or processing fees may apply, particularly if the de minimis treatment does not apply to your shipment. Any charges assessed by customs or the carrier are the buyer's responsibility unless stated otherwise.
- **EU buyers:** Import **VAT** applies to most shipments. Where we are registered for the **Import One-Stop-Shop (IOSS)**, VAT on consignments valued at **€150 or under** may be collected at checkout so your parcel clears customs without a surprise VAT bill. **Effective 1 July 2026, the EU's €150 customs-duty exemption is removed and a transitional flat customs duty (currently €3 per tariff line item) may apply to low-value parcels in addition to VAT.** Carrier handling fees may also apply. `[Confirm your IOSS registration status and update this section accordingly.]`
- **UK buyers:** Import VAT and, for consignments over £135, duties may apply; collection may occur at checkout or on delivery depending on value and our registration status.
- **Other countries:** Duties and taxes vary; please check your local customs rules.

We cannot predict or control customs charges, and we cannot mark shipments as "gifts" or undervalue them — doing so is illegal. Refused parcels due to unpaid duties may be returned or abandoned at the buyer's expense.

- Please double-check your shipping address at checkout. **We are not responsible for orders delayed, lost, or returned due to an incorrect or incomplete address provided by you.**

- If your tracking shows "delivered" but you cannot locate the package, please check with neighbors and your local carrier first, then contact us within `[7]` days.

## 8. Lost, Delayed, or Damaged Packages

- **In transit / lost:** If your order has not arrived within `[30]` days of the estimated delivery window and tracking shows no movement, contact us at `[SUPPORT EMAIL]`. We will investigate with the carrier and, where a package is confirmed lost, offer a **replacement or refund**.
- **Damaged in transit:** If your item arrives damaged, contact us within `[7]` days of delivery with photos of the item and packaging so we can arrange a replacement or refund. See our [Returns & Refunds Policy](./returns-refunds.md).
- **Multiple shipments:** Orders with multiple items may arrive in separate packages on different days, since items can ship from different suppliers. Don't worry if one part arrives first.
