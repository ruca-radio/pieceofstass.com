# Piece of Stass - On-Page SEO Specifications

## Global Rules
*   **Brand Scrubbing:** Absolutely NO trademarked brand names (e.g., Nike, LV, Rolex) in titles, meta descriptions, URLs, alt text, or H1s.
*   **Title Tag Length:** 50-60 characters.
*   **Meta Description Length:** 150-160 characters.
*   **Image Alt Text:** Descriptive, keyword-rich, NO BRAND NAMES. Format: `[style/color] [item type] [angle/detail]`. E.g., `White chunky platform sneaker side profile`.

## Page Type: Homepage (`/`)
*   **Title Tag Template:** `Piece of Stass | Raid The Stash | Aesthetic Fashion & Accessories`
*   **Meta Description Template:** `Shop the latest in clean girl, quiet luxury, and Y2K aesthetics. Unbranded, trend-forward apparel, footwear, and accessories. Raid the stash today.`
*   **H1 Rule:** Only one H1. Should encompass the brand and core value proposition (e.g., `<h1>Piece of Stass: Curated Aesthetic Essentials</h1>`).
*   **Schema.org Type:** `WebSite` (with SearchAction), `Organization`.
*   **Internal Linking:** Link to top-level category PLPs (Footwear, Bags, Watches, Apparel) and current trending collections (e.g., "Old Money Edit").

## Page Type: Category Listing Page (PLP)
*   **Title Tag Template:** `[Target Keyword] | Aesthetic [Category Name] | Piece of Stass`
    *   *Example:* `Chunky White Sneakers Women | Aesthetic Footwear | Piece of Stass`
*   **Meta Description Template:** `Discover our curated collection of [Target Keyword]. Shop unbranded, high-quality [Category Name] designed for the [Aesthetic e.g., clean girl] look. Free shipping over $50.`
*   **H1 Rule:** Exact match to primary category keyword or aesthetic theme (e.g., `<h1>Chunky White Sneakers</h1>`).
*   **Schema.org Type:** `ItemList`, `BreadcrumbList`.
*   **Internal Linking:** Link to sub-categories (if applicable). Link to related styling blog posts at the bottom of the description text.

## Page Type: Product Detail Page (PDP)
*   **Title Tag Template:** `[Product Title] | [Category Name] | Piece of Stass`
    *   *Example:* `White Cement High-Top Court Sneaker | Footwear | Piece of Stass`
*   **Meta Description Template:** `Shop the [Product Title]. [1-2 sentences of description highlighting aesthetic and key feature]. Perfect for your next [Aesthetic] outfit.`
    *   *Example:* `Shop the White Cement High-Top Court Sneaker. Structured retro style built for everyday outfits with a crisp athletic finish. Perfect for streetwear looks.`
*   **H1 Rule:** Exact match to the Product Title. (e.g., `<h1>White Cement High-Top Court Sneaker</h1>`).
*   **Schema.org Type:** `Product` (with Offers, aggregateRating), `BreadcrumbList`, `FAQPage` (if applicable).
*   **Internal Linking:** "You May Also Like" carousel linking to related products within the same aesthetic. Breadcrumbs linking back to PLP and Home.

## Page Type: Blog Post (`/blogs/*`)
*   **Title Tag Template:** `[Blog Title] | Piece of Stass Style Guide`
    *   *Example:* `How to Style Chunky Sneakers in 2026 | Piece of Stass Style Guide`
*   **Meta Description Template:** `[Engaging hook sentence]. Learn [Action/Value proposition of article]. Read the Piece of Stass guide to [Topic].`
*   **H1 Rule:** Engaging, keyword-optimized article title.
*   **Schema.org Type:** `Article`, `VideoObject` (if Anna L's TikToks are embedded).
*   **Internal Linking:** Aggressive inline linking (3-5 links per post) to relevant PDPs and PLPs using natural anchor text. Link to related blog posts.

## Page Type: About Page (`/pages/about`)
*   **Title Tag Template:** `About Us | Anna L & Piece of Stass`
*   **Meta Description Template:** `Learn the story behind Piece of Stass, founded by Anna L. We curate unbranded, aesthetic-driven fashion and accessories so you can raid the stash.`
*   **H1 Rule:** `<h1>About Piece of Stass</h1>`
*   **Schema.org Type:** `AboutPage`, `Organization`.
*   **Internal Linking:** Link to best-selling PLPs and the homepage.
