# Piece of Stass - 90-Day Content & SEO Calendar (Q1)

## Overview
This 90-day sprint focuses on establishing topical authority around "Quiet Luxury," "Coquette," and "Y2K" aesthetics, supporting the top-tier product categories. The strategy integrates SEO blogging with Anna L's social media presence and email marketing to drive traffic and user-generated content (UGC).

## Month 1: The "Quiet Luxury" & Basics Foundation
**Focus:** Establishing authority on timeless, unbranded staples to capture high-volume "old money" and "quiet luxury" searches.

*   **Week 1 (Launch Week):**
    *   **Blog Post:** Quiet Luxury vs. Old Money: What's the Difference in 2026?
    *   **Social Repurposing:** Anna L posts a TikTok/Reel breaking down the article's points, visually demonstrating the difference using PoS products. Link in bio to the blog.
    *   **Email (Klaviyo):** Welcome flow emphasis on "Unbranded Excellence."
*   **Week 2:**
    *   **Blog Post:** The Dropship Dilemma: Why Our Orders Take 10-20 Days (Transparency)
    *   **Social Repurposing:** "Behind the Scenes" style IG Story explaining the supply chain, fostering trust.
    *   **Internal Linking Focus:** Ensure the "Quiet Luxury" blog links heavily to foundational apparel PLPs.
*   **Week 3:**
    *   **Blog Post:** How to Build a Capsule Wardrobe on a Budget
    *   **Social Repurposing:** Anna L "Capsule Wardrobe Challenge" - 10 items, 15 outfits.
    *   **Email (Klaviyo):** Send out the Capsule Wardrobe guide. CTA: "Reply with your capsule must-haves!"
*   **Week 4:**
    *   **Blog Post:** Stealth Wealth: How to Look Expensive on a Budget
    *   **Internal Linking Focus:** Audit Month 1 posts. Add links from high-performing pages to new posts.
    *   **UGC Push:** Launch #RaidTheStash campaign on social. Encourage customers to post their styling for a chance to be featured.

## Month 2: Footwear Focus & The "Clean Girl" Aesthetic
**Focus:** Driving traffic to high-intent transactional keywords (Tier 1) like chunky sneakers and platform shoes.

*   **Week 5:**

*   **Week 6:**

    *   **Site Update:** Ensure this guide is linked near the 'Add to Cart' button on every footwear PDP to improve conversion rates.
*   **Week 7:**
    *   **Blog Post:** Step-by-Step Guide: How to Clean White Sneakers
    *   **Social Repurposing:** ASMR-style sneaker cleaning video on TikTok/Shorts (highly viral format).
*   **Week 8:**
    *   **Blog Post:** The Ultimate Clean Girl Aesthetic Guide: Makeup, Hair, and Outfits
    *   **Email (Klaviyo) UGC Send:** Send an email featuring the best #RaidTheStash customer photos focusing on footwear. Incentivize photo reviews with a discount code (crucial for SEO image search and PDP conversion).

*   **Week 11:**
    *   **Blog Post:** 5 Ways: How to Wear a Crossbody Bag Stylishly

## Ongoing Social-to-Blog Repurposing Flow (Weekly)
1.  **Monday:** Publish long-form Blog Post on Piece of Stass.
2.  **Tuesday:** Anna L records a short-form video (TikTok/Reels/Shorts) based on the blog's core premise.
3.  **Wednesday:** Post video across platforms. Use the caption to drive traffic to the full guide via Linktree/bio link.
4.  **Thursday:** Extract 3-5 key quotes or tips from the blog and create graphic carousels for Instagram.
5.  **Friday:** Embed the highest-performing TikTok video into the original Blog Post using the VideoObject schema to increase time-on-page and SEO performance.
