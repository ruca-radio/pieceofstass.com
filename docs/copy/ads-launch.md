## 5. Channel format notes (for the media buyer)

- **Meta Reels / TikTok In-feed:** Full UGC scripts above. 9:16, captions baked in, hook text in first frame.
- **Meta Stories / TikTok Spark:** 15s cuts (Scripts 1, 3, 5). Add a swipe-up/sticker CTA. Keep text out of bottom 250px (UI safe zone).
- **Meta Feed (static or short video):** Pair any Hook (1–10) as headline + any Body (1–10) as primary text + any CTA (1–10) as button. Suggested launch combo: Hook 1 + Body 1 + CTA 1.
- **Headline field (Meta, ≤40 char picks):** "The look for less, daily" · "Raid the stash" · "Stashed before it's trending"
- **Universal compliance:** No brand/model names in copy, captions, on-screen text, or thumbnails. No "dupe/replica/inspired-by." Review all visuals for visible logos before boosting (per `scrub-rules.md`).
