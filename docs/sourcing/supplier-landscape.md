## Category Deep-Dives

### Women's Fashion
**Top picks:** Trendsi (pure dropship, US warehouse, free), Tasha Apparel (same-day LA dispatch), Bloom Wholesale (US-made options, no MOQ), Spocket (premium curated catalog).

- Trendsi is the standout: free, Shopify + TikTok Shop native, 100k+ SKUs, 2–5 day US ship. Catalog is women's only, which aligns with Anna's core Gen-Z female audience.
- Tasha Apparel COGS are $7.50–$16/piece wholesale — exceptional for fast fashion. Same-day dispatch from LA. No subscription fee.
- Bloom Wholesale: US-made clothing available; flat-rate $9.95 shipping under $400 order.

### Men's Fashion
**Gap:** Trendsi, Tasha Apparel, and Bloom Wholesale do not carry men's. Primary options: Spocket (has men's), CJDropshipping (US warehouse men's basics), Syncee (multi-category), FashionGo (LA wholesale vendors with men's section).

### Kids' Fashion
**Options:** Spocket (has kids'), CJDropshipping (kids' category available in US warehouse), Syncee. Specialized kids' dropship is limited in pure-US-warehouse options — FashionGo vendor network covers this for wholesale.

### Watches (Legitimate Fashion Watches)
**Options:** WatchDropShip (authentic, 250+ models, EU-based, 5–10 day US delivery), CJDropshipping (fashion watches in US warehouse, 2–5 day delivery), EPROLO (watch category available). **Critical note:** Avoid any supplier listing watches with brand names (Rolex, AP, Cartier) as "inspired by" — that is still trademark infringement. Stick to generic/house-brand fashion watches only.

### Bags & Accessories
**Options:** Spocket (has bags/accessories), Trendsi (accessories add-on), CJDropshipping (wide accessories catalog), Syncee, AppScenic. Legitimate unbranded bags from US-warehouse suppliers available through Spocket and CJDropshipping.

### Fragrance (Legal — Generic/Unbranded Only)
**Key distinction:** "Fragrance dupes" sold as generic scents (no brand name, no trademarked bottle design) are legal. Selling products labeled "Chanel No. 5 dupe" is infringement.
- **Blanka**: Private-label beauty; no fragrance/perfume listed directly.
- **Supliful**: No fragrance listed.
- **Faire**: Several indie fragrance brands (unbranded/natural; check individual brand trademark status).
- **Wholesale generic fragrance** via Spocket or CJDropshipping (generic/house-brand): available but limited US-warehouse options.
- **Private-label perfume** (custom bottle + your branding, generic scent): Project Fragrance (250-unit MOQ), Wicked Good Perfume (18-unit MOQ, 10-day TAT), Alpha Aromatics (custom manufacturing). These are the cleanest legal path for a fragrance line.

### Tech Accessories
**Options:** CJDropshipping (US warehouse; phone cases, AirPod cases, cables — 2–5 day delivery), Spocket (tech accessories), AppScenic (electronics accessories), Syncee. **Avoid:** Any tech accessories that reference Apple, Samsung, or other brands by name/logo — trademark exposure.

### Print-on-Demand (Piece of Stass Branded Merch)
**Top picks:** Printful (Medusa plugin available on GitHub), Gelato (33-country production network), Printify (900+ products, largest partner network).
