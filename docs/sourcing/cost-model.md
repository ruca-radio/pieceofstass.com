## Methodology

**All-in delivered cost** = COGS (product) + Platform fee (amortized per order) + Shipping to customer  
**Gross margin** = (Retail price в€’ All-in COGS) / Retail price  
**Yupoo baseline** = 2.5вЂ“4x markup on wholesale price from Chinese supplier; 15вЂ“30 day shipping; existential legal + platform risk

Retail prices shown are recommended at a 2.5вЂ“3.5x markup over COGS for fashion/accessories, and 3вЂ“4x for beauty/fragrance (industry standard for DTC brands). Platform fees are amortized across an assumed order volume of 100 units/month per supplier.

### Key Takeaway

The new supplier stack delivers **equivalent or better gross margins** vs. Yupoo вЂ” while eliminating:
- Trademark infringement exposure
- Stripe account termination risk
- Meta/TikTok ad ban risk
- 15вЂ“30 day shipping (в†’ 2вЂ“5 days)
- US Customs seizure risk (19 U.S.C. В§1526)
- Payment friction (WeChat/WU в†’ Stripe)

The margin trade-offs (primarily on watches and bags, where Yupoo's high markups on replicas inflated apparent margins) are more than offset by: (a) fragrance private-label margin improvement, (b) POD merch as a new high-margin category, and (c) the elimination of the existential risk that Yupoo represents.
