# Private-Label Opportunity — Piece of Stass
*Moving Hero SKUs to Branded "Piece of Stass" Products*
*Sourcing & Supply Chain Analysis | June 2026*

---

## The Core Argument

Dropshipping Trendsi or CJDropshipping products makes money but builds nothing defensible. Any competitor can list the same Trendsi SKU tomorrow. Private-label is the only path from "influencer storefront" to "brand."

**Three compounding benefits of private-label:**

| Benefit | Dropship (Status Quo) | Private-Label |
|---------|----------------------|---------------|
| Gross margin | 40–55% | 60–75% |
| Brand equity | Zero (product belongs to supplier) | High (your brand, your SKU) |
| Defensibility | None (catalog clones exist) | High (exclusive product) |
| Trademark risk | Moderate (unbranded drops) | None (you ARE the brand) |
| Ad performance | Commodity pricing pressure | Premium positioning possible |
| Customer LTV | Low (no brand affinity) | High (customers buy "Piece of Stass") |

**The margin math:** A Trendsi top with COGS $12, retailed at $32 → **62.5% gross margin**. A Piece of Stass private-label top with COGS $8 (custom manufactured at 60 units MOQ via Trendsi Made-to-Order), retailed at $38 → **78.9% gross margin** + 0% repeat commodity competition.

---

## The 5 Hero SKU Candidates

These 5 SKU types are recommended based on: (1) volume potential from Gen-Z female audience, (2) ease of private-labeling, (3) margin opportunity, (4) brand-building value, and (5) low regulatory complexity.

---

### SKU 1: Piece of Stass Signature Fragrance

**The Opportunity**
Fragrance is the highest-margin beauty category (70–80% gross margin at retail). "Clean" and "vibe"-driven fragrance is one of the fastest-growing segments for Gen-Z. The Yupoo fragrance catalog (`jmshop88`) is a liability — private-label fragrance replaces it with a brand asset.

**Product Concept:** 2–3 signature scents under the Piece of Stass name. Minimalist bottle, clean aesthetic. Names like "Stass No.1" or seasonal names. Zero reference to any designer brand.

**Sourcing Path:**
| Manufacturer | MOQ | Lead Time | Unit COGS (est.) | Notes |
|-------------|:---:|:---------:|:---------------:|-------|
| **Wicked Good Perfume** | 18 units/fragrance | 10 business days | ~$8–$12 | Organic alcohol base; custom label; lowest MOQ available | 
| **Project Fragrance** | 250 units/SKU | ~3–4 weeks | ~$6–$9 | Curated scent library; ready-to-sell packaging |
| **Alpha Aromatics** | Custom quote | Custom | ~$5–$8 (at scale) | Full custom formula + bottle design; minimum ~$3k engagement |

**Recommended entry:** Start with Wicked Good Perfume at 18-unit MOQ to validate the concept ($150–$200 all-in for a test run), then move to Project Fragrance at 250 units once a winning scent is confirmed.

**Economics:**
- COGS (250-unit run): ~$7/unit
- Packaging (custom label + box): ~$2.50/unit
- Shipping to customer (Medusa fulfillment from 3PL): ~$4.50
- **All-in delivered cost:** ~$14
- **Retail price:** $38–$48
- **Gross margin:** 63–70%
- **vs. Yupoo fragrance** (branded replica, ~$15 COGS, $45 retail, ~67% gross but existential legal risk)

**Why now:** Fragrance has the highest content-to-conversion ratio on TikTok of any beauty category. A 30-second "scent reveal" video routinely drives 5–10x the views of a clothing haul.

### SKU 2: Piece of Stass Logo Hoodie & Tee (POD via Printful)

**The Opportunity**
Influencer brand merch is the lowest-risk private-label entry: zero MOQ, no inventory, immediate launch via Printful. A simple logo hoodie is the first touchpoint for audience-to-customer conversion — and it signals brand legitimacy.

**Product Concept:** Heavyweight French terry hoodie + fitted tee with Piece of Stass wordmark or logo graphic. 2–3 colorways (neutrals: sand, black, slate).

**Sourcing Path:**
- **Printful** — Bella+Canvas or Gildan heavy blend base
- No MOQ; print-on-demand per order
- Custom branded label (inside neck tag): +$2.49/label
- Embroidered logo option: +$2.95–$6.50 digitization one-time fee

**Economics:**
| Product | Printful COGS (base) | With Growth plan (33% off) | Retail Price | Gross Margin |
|---------|:--------------------:|:--------------------------:|:------------:|:------------:|
| Premium hoodie (Bella+Canvas) | ~$32 | ~$21 | $65–$75 | 67–72% |
| Classic tee (Bella+Canvas) | ~$18 | ~$12 | $35–$40 | 66–70% |

With Printful Growth at $24.99/mo, products cost up to 33% less — margins scale significantly.

**Launch path:**
1. Design wordmark logo (Anna already has brand identity)
2. Upload to Printful; connect via medusa-plugin-printful
3. Order 3–5 samples at 25% sample discount (Growth plan perk)
4. Photograph on Anna → TikTok launch video
5. Set retail and push live

**Why now:** Zero capital outlay. Generates brand equity immediately. Acts as anchor product that makes Piece of Stass feel like a real brand vs. a dropship aggregator.

### SKU 3: Piece of Stass Private-Label Beauty Set (via Blanka)

**The Opportunity**
Beauty is Anna's adjacent category — Gen-Z audiences expect their favorite influencers to have a beauty line. Blanka enables a fully private-labeled beauty launch in under 24 hours with zero MOQ.

**Product Concept:** "Piece of Stass Beauty" — 3-SKU starter set:
1. Tinted lip gloss (bestselling beauty item on TikTok)
2. Volumizing mascara
3. Hydrating face mist / setting spray

All products carry the "Piece of Stass Beauty" logo. Custom label via Blanka's brand setup.

**Sourcing Path via Blanka:**
- Growth plan: $39/mo → 10 branded products
- One-time brand setup fee (~$149–$249 per Blanka's historical pricing; confirm current)
- Zero MOQ — first sale on Day 1
- COGS per item (estimate based on Blanka's disclosed pricing model): ~$10–$15
- Blanka fulfills from US; 3–7 day delivery

**Economics:**
| Product | Blanka COGS (est.) | Retail Price | Gross Margin |
|---------|:-----------------:|:------------:|:------------:|
| Tinted lip gloss | ~$10 | $22–$28 | 55–64% |
| Mascara | ~$12 | $28–$35 | 57–66% |
| Face mist | ~$11 | $25–$32 | 56–66% |
| 3-piece set (bundle) | ~$33 | $65–$75 | 50–56% |

**Why now:** Blanka brands report average 190% markup from Blanka wholesale. Beauty is the highest-engagement TikTok category. A branded beauty line instantly elevates the store from "reseller" to "brand founder."

### SKU 4: Piece of Stass Signature Micro-Bag

**The Opportunity**
The bags category (Yupoo `3293950449`) is 🔴 CRITICAL risk — almost certainly LV/Gucci-adjacent. A private-label Piece of Stass micro-bag (no brand logo, unique design) is a defensible hero SKU that drives high AOV and viral content.

**Product Concept:** Quilted mini crossbody bag in 2–3 neutral/trendy colorways. Piece of Stass hardware logo tag or embossed logo on corner. Minimalist aesthetic — "quiet luxury" positioning for Gen-Z.

**Sourcing Path:**
- **Trendsi Made-to-Order (MTO):** MOQ 60 pieces; reorder lead time 7 days; Trendsi handles production, quality, US fulfillment
- **Alternatively:** Source via a US-based accessories private-label manufacturer (requires more upfront diligence; suggest Trendsi MTO for speed)

**Trendsi MTO Path:**
1. Browse Trendsi's custom/MTO catalog for bag base styles
2. Request sample (customization quote)
3. MOQ: 60 units per SKU
4. Lead time: ~7 days on reorder; longer on first production run
5. Custom label/tag with Piece of Stass branding

**Economics:**
| Scenario | COGS | Retail | Gross Margin |
|---------|:----:|:------:|:------------:|
| Trendsi MTO mini bag (60-unit run) | ~$18–$22 | $55–$75 | 60–71% |
| vs. Dropship equivalent (Spocket) | ~$20–$28 | $55–$75 | 49–64% |
| vs. Yupoo replica bag | ~$25 | $80–$120 | legal liability |

**Why now:** The micro-bag is a "hero SKU" — one viral video can sell out a 60-unit run in 24 hours. With Trendsi MTO's 7-day reorder lead time, inventory can be restocked quickly. The Piece of Stass logo/tag differentiates it permanently from generic dropship alternatives.

### SKU 5: Piece of Stass Scent-Branded Candle / Body Oil (adjacent beauty)

**The Opportunity**
Body oil and candles are the bridge between fashion and fragrance — highly giftable, high AOV, and ideal for TikTok aesthetic content. They also create a "signature scent" brand narrative that makes the fragrance line more coherent.

**Product Concept:**
- Piece of Stass body oil (3 signature scents, minimalist roller-bottle design)
- Piece of Stass soy candle (same scent lineup as the fragrance)
- Positioned as a "scent universe" — buy the fragrance, the candle, and the oil

**Sourcing Path:**
| Product | Manufacturer | MOQ | COGS (est.) | Notes |
|---------|------------|:---:|:----------:|-------|
| Body oil (roller) | Wicked Good Perfume or similar | 18–50 units | ~$5–$8 | Custom label; organic base |
| Soy candle | US-based candle private-label | 24–50 units | ~$6–$10 | Custom fragrance + label |

**Economics:**
| Product | COGS | Retail | Gross Margin |
|---------|:----:|:------:|:------------:|
| Body oil (30mL) | ~$7 | $24–$28 | 70–75% |
| Soy candle (8oz) | ~$9 | $28–$34 | 68–74% |
| Scent bundle (oil + candle) | ~$16 | $48–$58 | 67–72% |

**Why now:** Candles and body oils require no FDA registration unlike ingestible supplements. They are easily shipped, unbreakable (unlike glass perfume bottles in transit), and deliver high gross margins. The "scent universe" narrative turns a single fragrance concept into a 3–5 SKU product line.
