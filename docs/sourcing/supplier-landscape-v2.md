## Platform Safety Notes

All 13 new suppliers carry **zero replica / trademark risk** as evaluated:

- **Jewelry** (J. Goodin, Golden Stella, MMA Silver): own-house designs; no luxury brand references
- **Activewear** (Savoy Active, FashionTIY): unbranded; no sports-brand trademark exposure
- **Candles** (Candle Builders, Daniella's): your-label POD or private-label; zero infringement path
- **Fragrance** (Jubilee): private-label scent only; no branded bottle references
- **Nail** (Born Pretty, Beauty Big Bang): cosmetics tools/accessories; low trademark risk
- **Pet** (TopDawg, Mirage): authentic US products from 400+ legitimate US brands

**TikTok Shop compatibility:** Candle Builders (POD — ✅), TopDawg (US-sourced — ✅), J. Goodin (US-sourced — ✅), Savoy Active (US-sourced — ✅), Born Pretty (note: China-origin may require review on TikTok Shop). Recommend testing TopDawg and J. Goodin as TikTok Shop leads.
